32// Attribute includes
33// - ENDPOINT_MAX: max (logical) number of endpoint
34// - ENDPOINT_EXCLUSIVE_NUMBER: endpoint number with different direction IN and OUT aren't allowed,
35// e.g EP1 OUT & EP1 IN cannot exist together
36// - PORT_HIGHSPEED: mask to indicate which port support highspeed mode, bit0 for port0 and so on.
